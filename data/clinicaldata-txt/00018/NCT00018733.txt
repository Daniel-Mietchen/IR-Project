TITLE:
Biological Aspects of Depression and Antidepressant Drugs

CONDITION:
Depression

INTERVENTION:
paroxetine

SUMMARY:

      This study will be measuring changes in depressive symptoms over a 7 week time period.
      Double-blind placebo controlled trial using the pharmacologic agents Paroxetine or
      Desipramine.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 21 Years to 69 Years
Criteria:

        -  Diagnosis of major depressive disorder Ham-D Score > 18

          -  English speaking with ability to read and sign an informed consent

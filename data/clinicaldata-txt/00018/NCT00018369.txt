TITLE:
Age-Related Changes in Stress Hormone Regulation

CONDITION:
Physiological Processes

INTERVENTION:
NONE

SUMMARY:

      Studies investigating physiological processes involved in aging-related deficits in
      regulation of stress hormone systems.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 21 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  History of major psychiatric disorders,

          -  Neurologic disorder affecting cognitive function,

          -  Unstable medical problems,

          -  Medications including antidepressants, neuroleptics, anxiolytics, CNS stimulants,
             narcotic analgesics, anti-convulsants,

          -  Drugs effecting HPA axis function,

          -  Vision and hearing deficits,

          -  Undergoing major life event within one month or

          -  Loss of family member within 6 months.

TITLE:
Physiologic Effects of PRMS & Testosterone in the Debilitated Elderly

CONDITION:
Muscle Weakness

INTERVENTION:
exercise

SUMMARY:

      This study's primary objective is to determine the independent and combined effects of
      progressive resistance muscle strength training and testosterone on the development of
      sustainable improvements in physical function.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: Male
Age: 65 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

        The study will be limited to those patients who are metabolically stable and free of
        metastatic cancer and limiting end organ disease. Their total serum testosterone must be
        less than 480 and PSA less than 10.

TITLE:
Impact of Non-Commercialism Policy in Seattle Schools

CONDITION:
Obesity

INTERVENTION:
reducing exposure to marketing pressures

SUMMARY:

      This pilot study on the impact of a non-commercialism policy in Seattle schools will assess
      and evaluate obesity-related health outcomes of a real-life policy decision, recently voted
      on and adopted by the Seattle School Board. The policy reduces the exposure to advertising
      and other marketing pressures, including those from commercial food vendors.

NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 13 Years to 18 Years

        no restrictions

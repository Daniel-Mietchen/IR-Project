TITLE:
A 6-week Safety & Efficacy Study of Combination Intraocular Pressure-lowering Therapy in Patients With Open-angle Glaucoma or Ocular Hypertension

CONDITION:
Glaucoma, Open-angle

INTERVENTION:
Combination IOP Lowering Therapy

SUMMARY:

      To compare the intraocular pressure(IOP)-lowering efficacy of morning or evening
      instillations of a combination IOP-lowering therapy in patients with open-angle glaucoma or
      ocular hypertension.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Adult patients of any race and either sex with open angle glaucoma or ocular hypertension.

TITLE:
A 6-Week Safety and Efficacy Study of Travatan Compared to Xalcom in Subjects With Open-Angle Glaucoma(OAG) or Ocular Hypertension(OHT)

CONDITION:
Glaucoma, Open-Angle

INTERVENTION:
Travoprost (0.004%)

SUMMARY:

      To compare the safety and IOP-lowering efficacy of TRAVATAN and XALCOM in subjects with
      open-angle glaucoma or ocular hypertension.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Patients of any race and either sex with open-angle glaucoma (with or without pigment
        dispersion or pseudoexfoliation component) or ocular hypertension.

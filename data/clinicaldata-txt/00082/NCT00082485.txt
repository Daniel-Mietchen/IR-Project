TITLE:
Baclofen for the Treatment of Cocaine Dependence - 1

CONDITION:
Cocaine-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Baclofen

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to assess the efficacy and safety of baclofen for the treatment
      of cocaine dependence.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

      Double-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel design trial of baclofen for the treatment of
      cocaine dependence
    

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 99 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Subject must have DSM-IV diagnosis of cocaine dependence.

          -  Subject must be seeking treatment for cocaine dependence.

          -  Be be able to verbalize understanding of consent form, provide written consent and
             verbalize willingness to complete study procedures.

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Please contact site for more information.

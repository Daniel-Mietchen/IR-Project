TITLE:
Impact of Non-Commercialism Policy in Seattle Schools

CONDITION:
Obesity

INTERVENTION:
Water, Cafeteria and Staff Health Promotion

SUMMARY:

      This is a 3-year pilot study funded by National Institute of Health will assess and evaluate
      obesity-related health outcomes of a real-life policy decision, recently voted on and
      adopted by the Seattle School Board.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 14 Years to 18 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion: All students who attend Cleveland and Rainier Beach high schools are eligible.

        Exclusion: Those who are no longer attending those schools.

TITLE:
Motivational Enhancement for Spanish Speaking Individuals - 1

CONDITION:
Substance-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Behavior Therapy

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to compare a three-session Spanish version of Motivational
      Enhancement Therapy (MET) to standard treatment to see how well MET engages and retains
      Spanish-speaking clients in outpatient substance abuse treatment.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 99 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

        All subjects included unless medical/psychiatrically unstable

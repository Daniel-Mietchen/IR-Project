TITLE:
Motivational Enhancement Therapy to Improve Treatment Utilization in Pregnant Substance Users - 1

CONDITION:
Substance-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Behavior Therapy

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to compare Motivational Enhancement Therapy (MET) with standard
      drug abuse treatment among pregnant women.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: Female
Age: 18 Years to 99 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Pregnancy <30 weeks

          -  Have not attended drug treatment in past 28 days

          -  Not needing residential treatment (except detox)

          -  Not at risk for suicide/homicide

          -  Have no pending legal charges (except drug treatment)

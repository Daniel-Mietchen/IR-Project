TITLE:
Suboxone: (Buprenorphine/Naloxone) Comparison of Two Taper Schedules - 1

CONDITION:
Opioid-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Buprenorphine

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this protocol is to to compare the clinical utility of two dosage tapering
      regimens in Buprenorphine/Naloxone stabilized subjects for opiate detoxification.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 15 Years to 99 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Females are not pregnant or lactating

          -  Subjects meet DSM-IV criteria for opiate dependence, are medically and
             psychiatrically stable, and do not have a current history of benzodiazepine
             dependence abuse, dependence, or treatment

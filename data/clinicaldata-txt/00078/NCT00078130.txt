TITLE:
Buprenorphine/Naloxone - Facilitated Rehabilitation for Opioid Dependent Adolescents - 1

CONDITION:
Opioid-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Buprenorphine/naloxone

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to compare two 3-month treatments for adolescents/young adults
      who are addicted to heroin: buprenorphine/naloxone combined with psychosocial therapy and
      treatment as usual, a 7-14 day detoxification with buprenorphine and three weeks of
      psychosocial therapy.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 14 Years to 21 Years
Criteria:

        Male and non-pregnant female subjects seeking outpatient treatment for opioid dependence
        with physiological features without serious medical or psychiatric disorders.

TITLE:
Shoe Inserts for Treating Osteoarthritis of the Knee

CONDITION:
Osteoarthritis, Knee

INTERVENTION:
Shoe Insert

SUMMARY:

      This trial will test shoe inserts for the treatment of knee osteoarthritis, the most common
      form of knee arthritis. Those with disease on the inner (medial) aspect of the knee will be
      studied.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 45 Years to 85 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Medial knee osteoarthritis

          -  Pain

          -  No foot problems

          -  Lives in Boston, Massachusetts Metro area

TITLE:
Acupuncture Needling on Connective Tissue by Ultrasound

CONDITION:
Healthy

INTERVENTION:
NONE

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to investigate the effect of acupuncture needling on connective
      tissue using ultrasound techniques both in animals and in humans.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 55 Years
Criteria:

        -  Healthy Humans

          -  No bleeding disorders

          -  No anticoagulant therapy

          -  Cannot be pregnant

TITLE:
A Study of the Pharmacokinetics of Black Cohosh

CONDITION:
Hot Flashes

INTERVENTION:
Black cohosh

SUMMARY:

      Black cohosh is a natural supplement that has been used to treat and prevent menopausal
      symptoms. Remifemin is a standardized form of black cohosh. The purpose of this study is to
      determine the levels of components of black cohosh obtained in the blood of healthy women
      taking Remifemin.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

      As per Brief Summary
    

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: Female
Age: 18 Years to 70 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  No prior diagnosis of cancer other than non-melanoma skin cancer or carcinoma in-situ
             of the cervix

          -  No current prescription medication use

          -  Negative urine pregnancy test OR greater than age 50 with no menses for at least one
             year OR documented status post hysterectomy

          -  Competent to understand and sign informed consent

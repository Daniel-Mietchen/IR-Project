TITLE:
FFA Metabolism in Different Types of Human Obesity

CONDITION:
Obesity

INTERVENTION:
NONE

SUMMARY:

      We will give intravenous fat molecules to help us understand how the fat that is released
      from peoples fat cells is used. This will be done when people are eating, not eating and
      walking.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 50 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria: BMI 28-36 -upper body obesity and lower body obesity non-obese BMI
        19-24. Females premenopausal. Must be a non-smoker.

        Exclusion Criteria: BP> 160/95 on HTN meds, known systemic illness. Medications that
        affect fat metabolism. We do no cover travel expenses. Known heart and vascular disease.

TITLE:
Evaluation of Fanconi Syndrome and Cystinosis

CONDITION:
Cystinosis

INTERVENTION:
NONE

SUMMARY:

      OBJECTIVES:

      I. Classify renal tubular defects using clinical and biochemical findings in patients with
      Fanconi syndrome and cystinosis.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

      PROTOCOL OUTLINE:

      Patients receive a clinical and biochemical evaluation, including a psychometric assessment
      and molecular, renal, and thyroid studies.
    

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: N/A to N/A
Criteria:

        -  Inherited renal tubular defects, i.e., Fanconi syndrome

          -  Fanconi syndrome due to cystinosis eligible

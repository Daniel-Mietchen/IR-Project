TITLE:
A Comparison of Seroquel vs. Risperidone in Schizophrenia

CONDITION:
Schizophrenia

INTERVENTION:
quetiapine fumarate vs risperidone

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to show equal efficacy of both quetiapine and risperidone in
      subjects treated with study medication for up to 8 weeks.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 65 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria

          -  Male / Female, 18-65 years old

          -  Schizophrenia (as per DSM IV)

        Exclusion Criteria

          -  Concurrent use of psychotropic medication

          -  Does not fulfill moderately ill on CGI

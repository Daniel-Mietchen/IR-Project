TITLE:
Multicenter, Randomized, Double-Blind, Placebo Controlled, Efficacy Study on the Effects of Tolvaptan on Left Ventricular Dilatation in Congestive Heart Failure Patients

CONDITION:
Congestive Heart Failure

INTERVENTION:
Tolvaptan

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to study the effects of tolvaptan on the size and function of
      the left heart chamber (ventricle) in patients with congestive heart failure (CHF)
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        History of congestive heart failure

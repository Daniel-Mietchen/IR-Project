TITLE:
Computer-assisted Diabetes Self-management Interventions

CONDITION:
Diabetes Mellitus, Type 2

INTERVENTION:
Personalized Self-management

SUMMARY:

      This project evaluates the effectiveness of different aspects of a program to assist type 2
      diabetes patients with dietary changes. It evaluates the effectiveness of Personalized
      Self-Management Training and Community Resources support, using a randomized design. This
      study will also evaluate what percent of patients are willing to participate in such a
      program, and what percent of primary care physicians are willing to recommend it to their
      patients.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 30 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Type 2 diabetes

          -  Diagnosed for at least one year

          -  Patient of a primary care provider

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  No telephone

          -  Not speaking English

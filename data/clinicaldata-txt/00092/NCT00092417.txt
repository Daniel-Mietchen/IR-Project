TITLE:
Evaluation of the Safety and Tolerability of a Higher Potency Dose of an Investigational Vaccine Among Adults 50 Years of Age and Older (V211-009)

CONDITION:
Healthy

INTERVENTION:
Comparator: Varicella Zoster Virus Vaccine

SUMMARY:

      This study compared the safety and tolerability profile of a higher potency investigational
      vaccine to that of the investigational vaccine at a lower potency dose.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 50 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Healthy individuals 50 years of age or older with a history of chickenpox who have
             not had herpes zoster

        Exclusion Criteria :

          -  Preexisting conditions that might affect vaccine safety such as conditions that
             inhibit an immune response

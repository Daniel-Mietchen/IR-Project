TITLE:
Consistency Lots Vaccine Study (V260-009)

CONDITION:
Rotavirus Infections

INTERVENTION:
rotavirus vaccine, live, oral, pentavalent

SUMMARY:

      This study was designed to evaluate consistency in the antibody response to three
      manufactured lots of an investigational Rotavirus Vaccine.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 6 Weeks to 12 Weeks
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Healthy infants

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  History of abdominal disorders from a birth defect, intussusception, or abdominal
             surgery

          -  Known or suspected problems with the immune system

          -  Fever at time of immunization

          -  Prior administration of a rotavirus vaccine

          -  History of known prior rotavirus disease, chronic diarrhea, or failure to thrive.

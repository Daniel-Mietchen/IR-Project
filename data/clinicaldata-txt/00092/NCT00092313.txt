TITLE:
A Study of Two Approved Drugs in the Treatment of Postoperative Dental Pain (0966-182)

CONDITION:
Postoperative Pain

INTERVENTION:
MK0966, rofecoxib

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to compare the safety and effectiveness of two approved drugs
      in the treatment of pain following dental surgery.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

      The duration of treatment is 24 hours.
    

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 16 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Impaction of a molar tooth requiring removal

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Known allergy to the study drugs

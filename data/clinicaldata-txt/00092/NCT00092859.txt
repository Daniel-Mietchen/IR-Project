TITLE:
A 2-Year Study of an Investigational Drug in Obese Patients (0557-011)

CONDITION:
Obesity

INTERVENTION:
MK0557

SUMMARY:

      This is a 2-year study to assess the safety, tolerability, and efficacy of an
      investigational drug in obese patients.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Obese men and nonpregnant women at least 18 years of age with a specific body mass
             index (height and weight ratio) required by the study

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Patients with uncontrolled high blood pressure and/or diabetes mellitus (high blood
             sugar)

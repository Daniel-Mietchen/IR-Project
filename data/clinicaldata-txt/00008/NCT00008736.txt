TITLE:
Electrogastrography (EGC) in Premature Infants With Feeding Intolerance

CONDITION:
Infant, Newborn, Diseases

INTERVENTION:
metoclopramide

SUMMARY:

      Serial EGC measurements in premature infants attempting to correlate EGC measurements with
      signs of feeding intolerance and response to metoclopramide therapy.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: N/A to 1 Month
Criteria:

        Inclusion:

        Premature infants. Absence of 1) infection, 2) congenital anomalies, 3) growth retardation

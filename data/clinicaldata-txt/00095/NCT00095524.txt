TITLE:
Effects of Aripiprazole in Overweight Patients Treated With Olanzapine for Schizophrenia or Schizoaffective Disorder

CONDITION:
Body Weight Change

INTERVENTION:
Aripiprazole

SUMMARY:

      The primary purpose of this study is to compare the affects of aripiprazole and olanzapine
      on weight change.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 65 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Adult patients diagnosed with schizophrenia or schizoaffective disorder who are
             currently taking olanzapine.

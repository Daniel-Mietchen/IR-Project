TITLE:
Aripiprazole in Patients With Psychosis Associated With Parkinson's Disease

CONDITION:
Parkinson's Disease

INTERVENTION:
aripiprazole

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this clinical research study is to assess the safety and tolerability of
      aripiprazole in patients with psychosis associated with Parkinson's disease.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 50 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Primary diagnosis of idiopathic Parkinson's disease.

          -  Psychosis related to Parkinson's disease.

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Psychosis present prior to diagnosis of Parkinson's disease.

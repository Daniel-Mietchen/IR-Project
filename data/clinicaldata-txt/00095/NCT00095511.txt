TITLE:
Aripiprazole in Patients With Acute Mania

CONDITION:
Bipolar Disorder

INTERVENTION:
aripiprazole

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this trial is to test the safety & efficacy of aripiprazole in patients with
      Bipolar Mania for a period of at least 12 weeks.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Primary diagnosis of Acute Bipolar I Mania

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Patients with a DSM-IV diagnosis other than Bipolar I Mania

TITLE:
Intramuscular Aripiprazole in Acutely Agitated Patients Diagnosed With Dementia

CONDITION:
Dementia

INTERVENTION:
Aripiprazole

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this trial is to test the safety & tolerability of intramuscular aripiprazole
      in acutely agitated patients diagnosed with Dementia.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 55 Years to 95 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Acute agitation in Patients with a DSM-IV diagnosis of Alzheimer's, vascular or mixed
             Dementia.

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Current DSM-IV Axis 1 diagnosis other than Alzheimer's, vascular or mixed Dementia.

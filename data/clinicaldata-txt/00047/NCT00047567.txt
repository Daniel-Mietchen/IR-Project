TITLE:
Open-label Adjunctive Zonisamide for Bipolar Disorder

CONDITION:
Bipolar Disorders

INTERVENTION:
zonisamide

SUMMARY:

      To study the safety and efficacy of zonisamide as adjunctive therapy for children and
      adolescents with bipolar I or II disorder.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 6 Years to 17 Years
Criteria:

        -  Bipolar I/II

          -  Average intelligence

          -  Taking at least one, but no more than two mood stabilizers

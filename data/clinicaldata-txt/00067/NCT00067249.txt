TITLE:
Women's Use of Alternative Medicine: A Multiethnic Study

CONDITION:
Uterine Fibroids

INTERVENTION:
NONE

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to examine socio-cultural factors of womens use of
      complementary and alternative medicine (CAM). The effects of socioeconomic status, social
      networks and acculturation on CAM use will be assessed among white, African-, Mexican-, and
      Chinese-American women.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: Female
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion criteria:

          -  osteoporosis

          -  high cholesterol

          -  joint pain/arthritis

          -  insomnia

          -  uterine fibroids

          -  medically diagnosed depression

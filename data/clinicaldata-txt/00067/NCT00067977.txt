TITLE:
Clinical Trial to Slow the Progression of ADPKD

CONDITION:
Kidney, Polycystic

INTERVENTION:
anti-hypertensive medications

SUMMARY:

      This study will determine whether certain blood pressure medications will slow cyst growth
      or keep kidneys functioning longer in patients with autosomal dominant polycystic kidney
      disease (ADPKD). The study will take place at several sites in the United States. The start
      date of the study has not yet been determined.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 15 Years to 65 Years
Criteria:

        -  Previous diagnosis of autosomal dominant polycystic kidney disease by abdominal
             imaging

          -  Previous diagnosis of high blood pressure or current blood pressures above 130/80 mm
             Hg

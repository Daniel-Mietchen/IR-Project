TITLE:
Telephone Enhancement Procedure for Continuing Care - 1

CONDITION:
Substance-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Behavior Therapy

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to test a feasibility study of a telephone enhancement
      procedure (TELE) to improve participation in continuing care activities.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 99 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Residential substance abuse treatment patients who reside within the pre-specified
             catchment area who complete continuing care plans prior to discharge.

          -  Voluntary admit to residential treatment center; substance abuse or dependence;
             reside in targeted counties; and may be reached by telephone.

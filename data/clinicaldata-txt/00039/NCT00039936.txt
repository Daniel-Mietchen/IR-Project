TITLE:
Treatment of Binge Eating Disorder

CONDITION:
Bulimia

INTERVENTION:
Cognitive Behavior Treatment

SUMMARY:

      This is a multicenter (2 sites) randomized trial evaluating three group treatment models for
      subjects with binge eating disorder. One model is a cognitive behavior treatment (CBT)
      group, one is partial self help, and one is self help only.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        -  Meet binge eating disorder (BED) Criteria

          -  Not psychotic

          -  Not in other treatment

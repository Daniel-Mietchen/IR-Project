TITLE:
The Effects of Staggered Dosing on Interactions Between Paired Combinations of Nelfinavir, Ritonavir, and Saquinavir

CONDITION:
HIV Infections

INTERVENTION:
Ritonavir

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to see if staggering doses of nelfinavir, ritonavir, and
      saquinavir has any effect on the interactions between these drugs.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria

        Patients must have:

          -  Absence of HIV-1 infection as documented by any licensed ELISA test kit within 14
             days prior to study entry.

        Exclusion Criteria

        Co-existing Condition:

        Patients with the following symptoms and conditions are excluded:

          -  Any medical condition that, in the opinion of the investigator, would interfere with
             the patient's ability to participate in this protocol.

        Patients with the following prior conditions and symptoms are excluded:

          -  History of chronic illness such as hypertension, coronary artery disease, arthritis,
             diabetes, or any chronic gastrointestinal conditions that might interfere with drug
             absorption.

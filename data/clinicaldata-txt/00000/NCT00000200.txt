TITLE:
Cocaine Effects in Humans: Physiology and Behavior - 1

CONDITION:
Cocaine-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Methadone

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to compare the effects of buprenorphine or methadone
      maintenance on cocaine taking and on the physiological and subjective effects of cocaine,
      including cocaine craving, in opiate-dependent cocaine users.

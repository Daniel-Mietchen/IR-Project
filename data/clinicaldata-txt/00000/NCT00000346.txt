TITLE:
Cognitive Correlates of Substance Abuse - 11

CONDITION:
Amphetamine-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
counseling

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to characterize the cognitive performance of methamphetamine
      abusers by comparing them with cocaine abusers and normal controls.

NONE

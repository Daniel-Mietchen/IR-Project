TITLE:
Evaluation of Lofexidine for Treatment of Opioid Withdrawal - 3

CONDITION:
Opioid-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Lofexidine

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to determine the safety of lofexidine in the treatment of
      opiate withdrawal. Preliminary data will also be obtained to assess the ability of
      lofexidine to alleviate opiate withdrawal signs and symptoms.

NONE

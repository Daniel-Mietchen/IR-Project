TITLE:
High Dose Nimodipine Treatment Adjunct - 1

CONDITION:
Cocaine-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Nimodipine

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to determine if nimodipine is more effective than placebo in
      reducing stimulated craving for cocaine in cocaine dependent individuals denied access to
      cocaine in inpatient unit.

TITLE:
Serotonin/Dopamine Antagonism of Cocaine Effect: 2 - 2

CONDITION:
Cocaine-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Risperidone

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to evaluate use of risperidone with cocaine abusers. Study
      measures incorporate an appropriate integration of behavioral and neurobiological indices.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

      Risperidone in low doses is compared with placebo in a 6-week randomized trial. Subjects
      also receive supportive counseling. Outcome measure include rating of behavior, mood, drug
      abuse, global function, and urine toxicology.
    

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 50 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

        Cocaine abuse or dependence

        Exclusion Criteria:

        Dependence on other drugs. Major psychiatric illness

TITLE:
Safety and Effectiveness of Two Different Formulations of an HIV Vaccine in Infants Born to HIV-Infected Women

CONDITION:
HIV Infections

INTERVENTION:
Aluminum hydroxide

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to test the safety and effectiveness of two different
      formulations of an HIV vaccine in infants born to HIV-infected women.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: N/A to 3 Days
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria

        Infants may be eligible for this study if they:

          -  Are 1 to 3 days old.

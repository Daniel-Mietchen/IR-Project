TITLE:
Flupenthixol Decanoate in Methamphetamine Smoking - 1

CONDITION:
Substance-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Flupenthixol

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to evaluate the safety and efficacy of flupenthixol decanoate
      for treatment of methamphetamine dependence. Also, study will compare flupenthixol with
      desipramine in blocking methamphetamine self-administration.

NONE

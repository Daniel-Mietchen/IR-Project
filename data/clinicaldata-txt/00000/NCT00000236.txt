TITLE:
Buprenorphine Pharmacology Related to Addiction Treatment - 18

CONDITION:
Opioid-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Buprenorphine/naloxone

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to examine the interactions between buprenorphine and
      naltrexone, and to assess how they may directly impact the clinical issues involving:
      transferring patients from buprenorphine to naltrexone, developing a non-abusable form of
      buprenorphine, and enhancing patient acceptability of naltrexone.

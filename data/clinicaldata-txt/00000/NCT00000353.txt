TITLE:
Trial of Buprenorphine/Naloxone for Opiate Dependence - 2

CONDITION:
Opioid-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Buprenorphine/naloxone

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to determine the efficacy and safety of a
      buprenorphine/naloxone sublingual tablet formulation as an office-based therapy for opiate
      dependence treatment.

TITLE:
Temporal Discounting Delayed Outcomes on Opioid-Dependent Outpatients - 20

CONDITION:
Opioid-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Buprenorphine

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to assess the degree in which opioid-dependent outpatients
      discount the value of an additional maintenance dose of buprenorphine under differing states
      of opioid deprivation.

TITLE:
Alterations in Serotonergic Functions in Cocaine Addicts - 1

CONDITION:
Cocaine-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
M-CPP

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to evaluate alterations in the serotonergic system in cocaine
      addicts and attempt to dissociate changes secondary to cocaine use from those associated
      with premorbid characteristics.

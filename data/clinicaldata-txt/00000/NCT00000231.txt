CONDITION:

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to evaluate buprenorphine and behavioral treatment with and
      without contingent payment on compliance with pro-social activities. In addition, we will
      examine whether blind naltrexone dosing is effective in indicating naltrexone consumption.

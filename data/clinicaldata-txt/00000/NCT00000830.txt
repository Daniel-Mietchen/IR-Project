TITLE:
Intracellular Pharmacokinetics of Zidovudine Triphosphate in Maternal and Infant Cord Blood Mononuclear Cells.

CONDITION:
HIV Infections

INTERVENTION:
NONE

SUMMARY:

      To determine the levels of zidovudine triphosphate ( AZT-TP ) in maternal and fetal cord
      blood mononuclear cells. To determine the ratio of AZT-TP to endogenous nucleoside
      triphosphate levels in maternal and fetal cells. To determine the extent of drug transfer
      through the feto/placental unit.

NONE

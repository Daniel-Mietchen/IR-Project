TITLE:
Effects of Alcohol History on Effects of Sevoflurane and Nitrous Oxide - 13

CONDITION:
Alcohol-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
30% Nitrous oxide

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to evaluate the effects of alcohol history on the subjective
      and reinforcing effects of sevoflurane and nitrous oxide in healthy volunteers. All subjects
      underwent psychomotor testing during 4 sessions of placebo, drug/placebo, and choice of
      intervention.

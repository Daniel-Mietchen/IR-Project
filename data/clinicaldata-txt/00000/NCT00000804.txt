TITLE:
A Randomized Trial of L-735,524, An Inhibitor of the HIV Protease Enzyme, and Interleukin-2 in Persons Infected With HIV (NOTE: Only For Patients Who Previously Completed NIAID 93 CC-113)

CONDITION:
HIV Infections

INTERVENTION:
Indinavir sulfate

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this trial is to study L-735,524, which is an inhibitor of the HIV protease
      enzyme. It will be used with interleukin-2 in patients infected with HIV.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:
NONE

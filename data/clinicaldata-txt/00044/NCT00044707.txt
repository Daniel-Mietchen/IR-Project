TITLE:
Evaluation of an Orally Administered Medication When Taken in Conjunction With Pramlintide

CONDITION:
Diabetes Mellitus, Non-Insulin-Dependent

INTERVENTION:
Pramlintide acetate

SUMMARY:

      This is a randomized, single-blind, placebo-controlled, crossover study to examine the
      effect of pramlintide on the pharmacokinetics of an orally administered medication
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 60 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Type 2 diabetes mellitus treated with diet and/or oral agents

          -  HbA1c 6.5-11.0

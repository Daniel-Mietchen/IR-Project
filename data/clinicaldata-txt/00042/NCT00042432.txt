TITLE:
Study for Secondary Hyperparathyroidism in Chronic Renal Insufficiency Patients

CONDITION:
Secondary Hyperparathyroidism

INTERVENTION:
cinacalcet (AMG 073)

SUMMARY:

      This study will assess an investigational medication for patients with chronic renal
      insufficiency (pre-dialysis) who have secondary hyperparathyroidism.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Patients must have chronic renal insufficiency (pre-dialysis)

          -  Have below normal creatinine clearance

          -  Have elevated parathyroid hormone levels

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Pregnant or nursing

          -  Heart attack in the last 3 months.

TITLE:
A Study to Evaluate an Investigational Drug in Patients With Mixed Hyperlipidemia (0653A-071)(COMPLETED)

CONDITION:
Hyperlipidemia

INTERVENTION:
ezetimibe (+) simvastatin

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to assess the cholesterol lowering effects of an
      investigational drug in patients with mixed hyperlipidemia (high cholesterol and high
      triglycerides).
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 79 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Men and women 18-79 years of age with high cholesterol and high triglycerides.

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Individuals who do not meet specific cholesterol or triglyceride levels as required
             by the study.

TITLE:
Mechanism of Action of TRAVATAN 0.004% in Subjects With Glaucoma or Ocular Hypertension

CONDITION:
Glaucoma

INTERVENTION:
Travoprost

SUMMARY:

      The primary objective of this study is to describe the effect of TRAVATAN 0.004% Ophthalmic
      Solution on aqueous humor dynamics in subjects with a clinical diagnosis of open angle
      glaucoma (OAG) or ocular hypertension (OHT).
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        -  Subjects 18 years of age or older

          -  of either sex

          -  of any race

          -  diagnosed with ocular hypertension or open angle glaucoma

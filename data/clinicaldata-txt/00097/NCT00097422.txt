TITLE:
OPT-80 in Clostridium Difficile-Associated Diarrhea (CDAD)

CONDITION:
Diarrhea

INTERVENTION:
OPT-80

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to investigate the safety and perform preliminary clinical
      evaluation in patients with mild to moderate CDAD.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

      This is a dose-finding study to select a safe and efficacious dose of OPT-80. Patients will
      be evaluated for safety and efficacy and pharmacokinetic parameters will be investigated.
    

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Patients with CDAD

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Patients with severe CDAD

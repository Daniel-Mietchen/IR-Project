TITLE:
Pathophysiological Subtyping of Abnormalities in Cocaine Dependence - 9

CONDITION:
Cocaine-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Cocaine-Related Disorders

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to define pathophysiological subtypes of abnormalities in
      subjects with cocaine dependence.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

      The objective of this study is to define subgroups of patients with differential treatment
      outcome on the basis of differential baseline electrophysiological clusters.

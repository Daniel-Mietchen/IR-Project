TITLE:
Prepulse Inhibition of Startle in Cocaine Dependence - 7

CONDITION:
Cocaine-Related Disorders

INTERVENTION:
Amantadine

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to assess the effects of chronic cocaine use on the acoustic
      startle response and on gating of this response in humans.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

      The objective of this study is to assess the effects of chronic cocaine use on the acoustic
      startle response and on gating of this response in humans.
    

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: Male
Age: 18 Years to 60 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

        Cocaine dependence. Male. Right handed. Negative urine for cocaine

        Exclusion Criteria:

        Positive urine for cocaine. History of head trauma. Significant medical disease.

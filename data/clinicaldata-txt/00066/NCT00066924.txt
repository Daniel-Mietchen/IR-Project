TITLE:
Spirituality, Religiosity, and Immune Functioning

CONDITION:
Cancer

INTERVENTION:
NONE

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to determine whether religiosity and spirituality are related
      to immune functioning, as measured by interleukin-6 blood plasma level, among terminally ill
      cancer patients.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 21 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion criteria:

          -  Inpatient at Calvary Hospital

          -  Diagnosis of terminal cancer

          -  Score of 20 or above on Mini-Mental State Exam

        Exclusion criteria:

          -  Presence of a psychiatric disorder that would preclude the production of meaningful
             data

TITLE:
Adult Primary Insomnia Polysomnographic Study (0928-004)(COMPLETED)

CONDITION:
Primary Insomnia

INTERVENTION:
gaboxadol

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of the study is to test the safety and effectiveness of the Investigational Drug
      on insomnia.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 64 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Adult patients with a diagnosis of primary insomnia and in good physical and mental
             health

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Adult patients with other medical , neurological and sleep disorders

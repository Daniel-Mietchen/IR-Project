TITLE:
A Study of Aripiprazole in Patients With Bipolar I Disorder With a Major Depressive Episode

CONDITION:
Bipolar I Disorder

INTERVENTION:
Aripiprazole

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to evaluate several doses of aripiprazole in patients with
      bipolar depression.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 65 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Men and women, ages 18-65

          -  Have experienced a prior manic episode that required treatment with a mood stabilizer
             or antipsychotic, and now meet criteria for a major depressive episode.

TITLE:
Broad Effectiveness: Study With Aripiprazole

CONDITION:
Schizophrenia

INTERVENTION:
Aripiprazole

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this study is to learn if aripiprazole is effective in the treatment of a
      large number of persons diagnosed with schizophrenia or schizoaffective disorders
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to 75 Years
Criteria:

        -  Patients with Schizophrenia or Schizoaffective Disorder

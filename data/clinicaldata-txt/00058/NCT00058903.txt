TITLE:
Treatment of Cholestatic Pruritus With Sertraline

CONDITION:
Pruritus

INTERVENTION:
sertraline

SUMMARY:

      The goal of this study is to find an effective and well-tolerated medical therapy for
      itching due to liver disease. Persons with primary biliary cirrhosis or chronic hepatitis C
      are currently being enrolled in the study. Persons participating in the study are given
      sertraline, a medication which is also often used for depression,to treat their itching. The
      dose is gradually increased as the effect on itching and any other potential side effects
      are carefully monitored.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 21 Years to 80 Years
Criteria:

        -  Itching for at least 3 months

          -  Primary biliary cirrhosis or hepatitis C

          -  Elevated alkaline phosphatase for at least 6 months

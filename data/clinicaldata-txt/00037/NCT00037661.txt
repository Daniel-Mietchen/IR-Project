TITLE:
Study of INS365 Ophthalmic Solution in a Controlled Adverse Environment in Patients With Dry Eye

CONDITION:
Keratoconjunctivitis Sicca

INTERVENTION:
INS365 Ophthalmic Solution

SUMMARY:

      Comparative efficacy trial of INS365 Ophthalmic Solution and placebo in patients with dry
      eye.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  have Non-Sjogren's associated dry eye disease with symptoms lasting greater than 6
             months

          -  had intermittent or regular artificial tear use within past 3 months

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  had LASIK surgery

          -  had punctal occlusion or cauterization within last 3 months

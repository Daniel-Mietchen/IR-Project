TITLE:
Six Month Clinical Research Study for Patients With Moderate or Severe Dry Eye Syndrome

CONDITION:
Keratoconjunctivitis Sicca

INTERVENTION:
Ophthalmic Emulsion

SUMMARY:

      A six-month clinical research trial to evaluate the effectiveness of an investigational
      medication for the treatment of dry eye syndrome in patients that have been diagnosed with
      moderate to severe dry eye syndrome, an autoimmune disorder AND/OR females 65 years of age
      or older.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        -  Eighteen years or older

          -  Diagnosis of moderate to severe dry eyes for at least 6 months and meet one or all of
             the following criteria:

          -  Diagnosis of autoimmune disorder

          -  Female 65 years of age or older

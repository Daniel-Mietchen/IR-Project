TITLE:
Study of BMS-275183 in Patients With Pre-treated Non-small Cell Lung Cancer

CONDITION:
Non-small Cell Lung Cancer

INTERVENTION:
BMS-275183

SUMMARY:

      The purpose of this clinical research study is to learn if BMS-275183 can shrink or slow the
      growth of the cancer in subjects with non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC). The safety of this
      treatment will also be studied.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 18 Years to N/A
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Advanced or metastatic pretreated NSCLC

          -  Measurable disease

          -  Adequate hematologic, hepatic and renal functions

          -  ECOG Performance Status of 0-2

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Inability to swallow capsules

          -  Recent significant cardiovascular disease

          -  Woman who are pregnant or breastfeeding

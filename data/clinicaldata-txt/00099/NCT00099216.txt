TITLE:
Efficacy and Safety of Rivastigmine Capsules in Patients With Probable Vascular Dementia

CONDITION:
Vascular Dementia

INTERVENTION:
Rivastigmine

SUMMARY:

      The goal of this research study is to evaluate the effectiveness, tolerability and safety of
      rivastigmine capsules in males and females between the ages of 50 and 85 years old with
      probable vascular dementia.
    

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
NONE

ELIGIBILITY:
Gender: All
Age: 50 Years to 85 Years
Criteria:

        Inclusion Criteria:

          -  Between the ages of 50 and 85 years old

          -  Have contact with a responsible caregiver 3 or more days per week

          -  Be male or a female who is surgically sterilized or one year post menopausal

        Exclusion Criteria:

          -  Current diagnosis of severe or unstable cardiovascular or other diseases

          -  Current diagnosis of active, uncontrolled seizure disorder, Parkinson's disease or
             Alzheimer's disease
